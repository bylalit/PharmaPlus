Our Origins

Founded by Doctors, Built for Patients.

In 2020, our founders realized that traditional pharmacies were failing patients with chronic illnesses. Wait times were long, inventory was poorly managed, and the prices were unnecessarily high due to intermediaries.

PharmaPlus acts as a direct bridge between certified GMP manufacturers and you. We built our own cold-chain logistics network to ensure that biologics, insulins, and highly sensitive compounds arrive safely.
